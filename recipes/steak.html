Pan-seared Beef Steak

Description

This is a great dish for meat lovers

Also, quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Sirloin beef cut
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Butter

    • Steps

    1. Pat the steak dry
    2. Season it
    3. heat the pan
    4. add olive oil
    5. Put steak in pan
    6. Sear and turn occasionally
    7. baste
    8. rest
